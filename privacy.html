Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

1. Introduction

Nexus Wallet ("we", "our", "us") is a non-custodial cryptocurrency wallet browser extension and mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our services.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

Nexus Wallet is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Private keys or seed phrases
  • Personal identification information
  • Transaction history
  • IP addresses or location data
  • Browsing history or activity
  • Email addresses or contact information

3. Local Data Storage

All wallet data, including encrypted private keys and user preferences, is stored locally on your device using the browser's built-in storage APIs. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

4. Third-Party Services

Nexus Wallet interacts with the following third-party services to provide functionality:

  • Solana RPC Nodes: To broadcast transactions and fetch on-chain data (balances, token info).
  • Jupiter API: To provide token swap quotes and execute swaps.
  • Price APIs: To fetch real-time token price data.
  • Onramp Providers: When you choose to purchase cryptocurrency, you may be redirected to a third-party service (e.g., MoonPay) which has its own privacy policy.

These services receive only the minimum information necessary (such as your public wallet address) to function. We do not share any additional personal data with these providers.

5. Encryption

Your private keys are encrypted using your password with industry-standard encryption algorithms before being stored locally. Your password is never stored — only a derived key is used for encryption and decryption.

6. Permissions

The browser extension requests only the minimum permissions necessary to operate:

  • Storage: To save encrypted wallet data locally.
  • Alarms: For auto-lock functionality.

7. Children's Privacy

Nexus Wallet is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
